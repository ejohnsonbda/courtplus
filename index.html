More than a tennis stadium.

The W.E.R. Joell Tennis Stadium is Bermuda's only national tennis stadium, operated by the Department of Sports and Recreation. It is a hub for sport, community, and island life.

Whether you are a seasoned competitor, a weekend player, or picking up a racket for the first time, CourtPlus makes it easy to book, play, and pay.

  • Bermuda's only national tennis stadium
  • Tennis and pickleball for all skill levels
  • Online booking via CourtReserve platform
  • Membership options for regular players
  • Junior development and adult programmes
  • Competitive leagues and tournaments
