Bermuda's Premier Tennis & Pickleball Facility

The W.E.R. Joell Tennis Stadium is a public sports facility operated by the Department of Sports and Recreation. Located in the heart of Bermuda, the stadium provides world-class tennis and pickleball courts to the community, competitive players, schools, and visiting athletes from around the world.

With a rich history in Bermuda's sporting heritage, the stadium offers a full range of hard and clay tennis courts alongside dedicated pickleball courts — all maintained to the highest standards. Whether you are a first-time recreational player or a seasoned competitor, W.E.R. Joell Tennis Stadium is your home for court sports in Bermuda.

Registration and booking is free via the CourtReserve platform. Simply create your account, book your court, and complete payment through the WER Joell payment portal.
